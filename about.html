About me

Background

Computer Science BSc graduate from Brunel University whom is diversified in roles such as Programming, Video Editing and graphic design

Currently undertaking an MA in Game Development at Falmouth University with an expected graduation date of 2023. Currently working as a technical artist at FireSlug studios to develop a 3D RPG dungeon crawler. As of recently have been apart of releasing the combat demo beta with regular weekly updates.

As for side projects, I have published a 2D platformer title onto Itch.io which has amassed over 275 downloads since April 2021. I am also working on my first major indie first person story/exploration game "Forgotten Memories" with the plan to publish it to steam.

I have programming experience in languages such as Java, C#, R, SQL, Python and HTML. Have been awarded an Academic excellence scholarship throughout my time at my undergraduate studies from the Physical sciences college. Having also been awarded an A+ (First) in my final year dissertation project, which is the design and implementation of a 3D serious Video game within Unity (C#) to simulate hazard perception in an urban environment for older adults.

I also have certifications in Information Technology/Computing at City and Islington College, A Level 2 BTEC Computing certification with a double Distinction grade being the highest grade possible. Not to mention also have a Level 3 extended Diploma BTEC certification in Information technology with the grade Distinction*Distinction*Distinction.
